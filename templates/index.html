How to use:

  1. 1. Upload BOM PDF - Your BOM file containing items and NSNs.
  2. 2. Upload DD1750 Template PDF - Your blank DD1750 template.
  3. 3. Click Generate - The app will extract items and fill them into the table.
  4. 4. Download DD1750.pdf - Save the generated file.
  5. 5. Open DD1750.pdf in Adobe Acrobat Pro - Open the downloaded file. The admin section (top) has editable form fields. The table section (bottom) has the items printed directly.
