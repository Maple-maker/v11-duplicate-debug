DD1750 Converter

How to use:

Upload your BOM PDF and DD1750 Template. Click Generate. The app will extract items and create a filled DD1750 form.

  • Step 1: Upload BOM PDF
  • Step 2: Upload DD1750 Template PDF
  • Step 3: Click Generate
  • Step 4: Download DD1750.pdf
  • Step 5: Open DD1750.pdf in Adobe Acrobat Pro
  • Step 6: Fill in admin fields (Unit, Packed By, etc.)
  • Step 7: Save

Note: The app prioritizes "OH QTY" (On Hand Quantity) over "AUTH QTY" (Authorized Quantity) for maximum accuracy.
